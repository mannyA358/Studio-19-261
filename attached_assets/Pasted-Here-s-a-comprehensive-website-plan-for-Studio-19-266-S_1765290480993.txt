Here's a comprehensive website plan for Studio 19:266:

## **STUDIO 19:266 - WEBSITE PLAN**

### **Design Philosophy**
Modern, sleek, and elegant with alternating black and white sections. Primary colors: black and white with gold (#d4af37) and blue (#1e90ff) accents. Clean, professional, luxury aesthetic throughout.

---

### **HEADER/NAVIGATION**
- **Logo Placement**: Center of navigation bar
  - "Studio 19:266" in gold lettering
  - Modern, minimalist design
  - Sticky header that stays at top while scrolling

- **Left Navigation (3 tabs)**:
  1. **Home** - Links to hero section
  2. **Services** (with dropdown hover menu)
     - Photography
     - Video Editing
  3. **Pricing** - Direct link to pricing section

- **Right Navigation (3 tabs)**:
  1. **About Us** - Studio background and team
  2. **Contact** - Contact form and info
  3. **Gallery** - Portfolio showcase

- **Styling**: Black background (#0a0a0a) with white text, gold hover effects, smooth transitions

---

### **SECTIONS (Alternating Black/White)**

**1. HERO SECTION (Black)**
- Large, impactful headline: "Capture Your Moments in Luxury"
- Subheading with gold accent color
- Call-to-action button (gold with hover effect)
- Subtle animated background elements
- Modern typography with wide letter spacing

**2. SERVICES SECTION (White)**
- Grid layout showcasing main services
- Cards with gold and blue gradient top border
- Hover effects: lift up, shadow glow
- Include: Portrait Photography, Event Coverage, Product Photography, Video Production, Editing Services, Commercial Shoots
- Each card has icon, title, and brief description

**3. PORTFOLIO/GALLERY SECTION (Black)**
- 4-column grid layout
- Showcase photography and videography work
- Hover effects: gold border, scale up
- Professional image placeholders with gradient overlays
- View Gallery button with gold styling

**4. PRICING SECTION (White)**
- 3 tiered pricing cards (Basic, Professional, Premium)
- Gold borders on cards
- Clear package details
- "Book Now" buttons with gold styling
- Transparent pricing with no hidden fees

**5. ABOUT US SECTION (Black)**
- Team introduction with photos
- Studio story and mission statement
- Why choose Studio 19:266
- Professional, welcoming tone
- Team member cards with subtle blue/gold accents

**6. TESTIMONIALS SECTION (White)**
- Client reviews and ratings
- 3-4 featured testimonials
- Star ratings in gold
- Client photos/names
- Smooth carousel or grid layout

**7. WHY CHOOSE US SECTION (Black)**
- 6 feature blocks
  - Professional Equipment
  - Expert Team
  - Quick Turnaround
  - Competitive Pricing
  - Custom Packages
  - Luxury Experience

**8. CONTACT SECTION (White)**
- Contact form with fields: Name, Email, Service Type, Message
- Contact information: Phone, Email, Address
- Google Map embedded
- Social media links in gold
- Professional styling with blue/gold accents

---

### **FOOTER (Black)**
- Logo and brief studio description
- Quick links navigation
- Contact info
- Social media icons (Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, TikTok)
- Copyright and privacy policy
- Gold accent dividers

---

### **INTERACTIVE FEATURES**
- Smooth scrolling between sections
- Hover effects on all interactive elements
- Dropdown menu for Services with smooth animation
- Form validation on contact page
- Image zoom on gallery hover
- Sticky navigation header
- Back-to-top button (subtle, gold)

---

### **COLOR SCHEME**
- **Primary**: Black (#0a0a0a), White (#ffffff)
- **Accent 1**: Gold (#d4af37)
- **Accent 2**: Blue (#1e90ff)
- **Text**: Dark gray (#333) on white, white on black
- **Backgrounds**: Light gray (#f5f5f5) for subtle sections

---

### **TYPOGRAPHY**
- **Headings**: Bold, modern sans-serif, wide letter-spacing
- **Body**: Clean, readable sans-serif (Segoe UI, Roboto, or similar)
- **Font Sizes**: Large headlines (42-56px), readable body text (14-16px)

---

### **ADDITIONAL FEATURES**
- **Mobile Responsive**: Full adaptation for tablets and phones
- **Fast Loading**: Optimized images and clean code
- **SEO Ready**: Proper meta tags and structure
- **Booking Integration**: Calendar or booking system
- **Email Newsletter**: Subscribe form in footer
- **Blog Section**: Photography tips and studio updates
- **FAQ Section**: Common questions about services
- **Virtual Tour**: 360-degree studio walkthrough
- **Before/After Gallery**: Show editing transformations

This plan creates a sophisticated, professional website that reflects the luxury and quality of Studio 19:266 while maintaining excellent user experience and navigation.
